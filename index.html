About Me

Hello! I am Andrew and I’m a full stack developer, using mostly React and Node. I’ve also been an educator and worked in the customer service industry for the past 10 years, so I know the importance of staying organized and in constant communication with the team and client. Mastering these things helped me be a reliable asset to my team.

Now I am a full stack software engineer and eager to use my skills to make fun and innovative applications for the world! I love the feeling of solving a particularly hard puzzle, and the sense of satisfaction in a completed project is unlike anything I've experienced in the customer service industry. Having something tangible to show off to my friends or family (or future employers) is also particularly attractive.

I am also a big fan of table top games and lore building, so as you can imagine, I have many project ideas ranging from virtual table top simulators to lore content creation. There are so many different applications for my new programming knowledge, and I am very excited to dive in and make all the ideas in my head into something real.
