About Me

Hello! I am Andrew and I am now chasing my career dreams! I remember back in the myspace days of spending hours and hours to customize your page and making every little detail pixel perfect. I used to feel so excited when I learned a new trick or shortcut and I'd want to show off to my friends, how I made the text scroll off the page or a small dancing animation.

Now I am a full stack software engineer and eager to use my skills to make fun and innovative applications for the world! I love the feeling of solving a particularly hard puzzle and the sense of satisfaction in a completed project is unlike anything I've experienced in a customer service industry. Having something tangible to show off to my friends or family (or future employers) is also particularly attractive.

I am also a big fan of table top games and lore building, so as you can imagine, I have many project ideas ranging from virtual table top simulators to lore content creation. There are so many different applications for my new programming knowledge and I am very excited to dive in and make all the ideas in my head into something real.
